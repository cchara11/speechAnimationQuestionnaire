Welcome to the Audio-driven Speech Animation for Virtual Characters questionnaire! The purpose of the questionnaire is to evaluate the naturalness of different speech animation approaches driven by audio input. Your task is to watch a series of videos of a male and a female virtual character and select the best one based on your own preference. More instructions will be given in the next pages.

The questionnaire takes about 25 minutes to complete. Please note that your participation is anonymous and you certify that you are at least 18 years old.
